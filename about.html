Who We Are

Green Chepalungu is a community-based organization committed to environmental conservation, tree planting, and sustainable living. Our mission is to make Chepalungu greener and healthier for generations to come.

Our Mission

To conserve the environment through reforestation, environmental awareness programs, and engaging the community in green practices.

Our Vision

A thriving, sustainable Chepalungu where people live in harmony with nature.

More About Us

Since our founding, we have planted thousands of trees, worked with schools to promote eco-friendly practices, and partnered with local leaders to support sustainable development initiatives. We believe every small action contributes to a larger, greener goal.
